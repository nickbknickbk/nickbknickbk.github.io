University of the Arts London Creative Computing Insitute: Information for Visiting PhD students

We welcome applications to visit the Creative Computing Insitute (CCI) as a visiting PhD student. Visits can be either 3 or 6 months, and are an extra part of your research degree study (the visiting programme is not accredited and will not constitute part of your current course). Whilst at CCI you should be undertaking a piece of research relating to your PhD and so you need to identify and contact a suitable potential supervisor in CCI to agree what research you would undertake during your visit to CCI.

Please see the following for more information:
  • Detailed guidance on the visiting PhD programme: pdf.
  • Details of the visiting PhD application process: pdf.
  • Basic details for how to apply including fees towards the end of the page: https://www.arts.ac.uk/research/phd-and-mphil-degrees

All potential applicants or enquirers should contact the UAL Research Degrees team directly at researchdegrees@arts.ac.uk to check that your application would be appropriate for this programme.
