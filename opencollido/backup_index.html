opencollido

Collidoscope is a two person granular synth that you can build yourself. With Collidoscope, you can record, mix and play with real-world sounds. Super fun, super cool.

Follow us on Twitter @opencollido

Get The Files

Software: All the software that you need to make your own Collidoscope right here. Pre-compiled for a quick start on Raspberry Pi, or grab the source code.

PCB designs: All the designs you need to make your own printed circuit boards (PCB) for using Teensy in your own Collidoscope.

Engineering designs: All the engineering design files you need to 3D print, laser cut, and CNC your own Collidoscope. Includes diagrams and layouts for how to build your own special Collidoscope sliders and sensors.
